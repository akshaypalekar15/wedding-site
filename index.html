Our. Love. Story

💫First Meet

We first met on the 9th of November, 2024, on what was meant to be a simple, casual date — just two people getting to know each other.
The setting was a cozy bistro café in Pune, with soft music in the background and a bit of nervous excitement in the air. What started as an arranged introduction quickly turned into a heartfelt conversation, filled with easy laughs, shared values, and a quiet connection that neither of us expected.
Somewhere between the coffee and the conversation, we knew — something special had just begun.

💍He Proposed

Our story had been unfolding beautifully — with time, trust, and countless memories. But the most unforgettable moment came on 7th February, during our ring exchange ceremony.
In the middle of all the love and laughter, he took a moment to make it official — not just with a ring, but with a heartfelt proposal that was simple, sincere, and straight from the soul.
With friends and family witnessing the moment, it felt perfect — a beautiful blend of tradition and a promise that we’ll hold close forever. And of course… she said yes, again — with the same smile and the same sparkle in her eyes.

💖Love Story

From awkward first texts to late-night calls filled with laughter, our story unfolded gently and beautifully.
We bonded over the little things — favorite foods, shared values, dreams of travel, and the joy of simply being real with one another.
There were no dramatic plot twists, just two people growing closer with every passing day, learning, supporting, and loving more deeply.
Our story isn’t just ours anymore — it’s something we’re excited to celebrate with all of you.

💒Wedding Day

Surrounded by the warmth of our families, the laughter of our friends, and blessings from all who love us — we’re tying the knot!
As we step into this beautiful new chapter, we’re filled with gratitude and joy. This isn’t just a celebration of love — it’s a celebration of everyone who helped us get here.
We can’t wait to share this special day with you — to dance, to celebrate, and to make memories that will last a lifetime. See you at the wedding!
